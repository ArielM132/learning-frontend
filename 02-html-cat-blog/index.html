About the Author

Hi there! I'm Ariel, an aspiring developer and tech enthusiast who finds endless inspiration in the curiosity of my beloved cat, Mr. Whiskers.

His boundless energy keeps me on my toes, and his ability to get into trouble (and cardboard boxes) taught me that perseverance is key, both in life and in coding.

Recent Posts

The Arrival: First Day Home

Mr. Whiskers arrived as a tiny ball of fur. I remember he spent his first few hours hiding under the sofa, observing everything with giant eyes. It was the beginning of a great friendship and the end of peace for my curtains.

That day I learned that gaining a cat's trust requires patience, a quality I now apply every time I face a bug in the Git terminal.

The Water Battle: First Bath

They say cats don't like water, and Mr. Whiskers confirmed the rule. What started as an attempt at hygiene ended in a chase all over the living room.

Despite the chaos, we managed to come out unscathed. It was a lesson in being prepared for the unexpected, much like when a production deployment doesn't go as planned.

A Year of Mischief: First Birthday

We celebrated his first year with a tuna "cake" and a new toy that he completely ignored to play with the wrapping paper instead. It's incredible how a small animal can change the dynamic of a home.

Today, while I study programming, he usually sits on my keyboard, reminding me that it's always a good time to take a break and play.
